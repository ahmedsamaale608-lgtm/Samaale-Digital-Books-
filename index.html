Soo Geli Buugaaga

Qaybtan waxaa keliya oo geli kara maamulaha bogga (admin-ka). Marka buug la geliyo, wuxuu si toos ah ugu soo muuqan doonaa maktabadda kor ku xusan — laakiin biraawsarkan (browser) oo keliya, sidaas darteed isticmaal isku mid ah marka aad buugaag ku darayso.

🔒 Foomka soo gelinta buugaagta waxaa gaar u ah maamulaha bogga oo keliya. Booqdayaasha kale waxay heli karaan oo keliya inay maktabadda arkaan oo ay buugaagta ka soo dejiyaan.
Halkan ku tuur sawirka qaabka (JPG), ama guji si aad u doorato
Ikhtiyaari — haddii aan sawir la gelin, buugga wuxuu heli doonaa qaab midab ah oo toos ah. Ugu badnaan 800KB.
Halkan ku tuur faylka, ama guji si aad u doorato
Ugu badnaan 1.5MB — faylasha waaweyn isticmaal xiriiriye (link) halkii.
✎ Waxaad wax ka beddelaysaa buug hore loo geliyay. Faylka iyo sawirka hore way sii jirayaan haddii aadan mid cusub soo gelin.
⚠️ Fiiro gaar ah: buugaagta aad soo gelisid waxay ku kaydsanaan doonaan biraawsarkan (browser) oo keliya — kuma muuqan doonaan booqdayaasha kale ee bogga, maadaama boggani uu isticmaalayo localStorage.
